Take Control of Your Finances with Own Your Money!
Your complete guide to budgeting, saving, investing, and building a financial routine that works for you.

Welcome to a journey toward financial empowerment! In *Own Your Money*, Michela provides practical tools and strategies for managing your money effectively. With step-by-step guidance, you’ll learn how to create budgets, save strategically, and invest wisely — all while building confidence in your financial future.

Master the Art of Budgeting

From the 50/30/20 rule to zero-based budgeting and the 3-bucket system, this book introduces flexible budgeting methods that suit your lifestyle. Say goodbye to the dreaded "B-word" and learn to control your finances without stress.

Smart Saving and Guilt-Free Spending

Saving is easy when it’s SMART (Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound). Michela also guides you on spending purposefully, helping you align your purchases with your values and priorities — guilt-free, even on small indulgences like fancy coffee.

Invest with Confidence

Understand the essentials of investing, from 401ks and IRAs to index funds and target date funds. Learn the importance of diversification and how to grow your wealth over time with a solid foundation in investment principles.

Build a Career You Love and Boost Your Earning Potential

Explore career development strategies, including finding fulfilling work, effective networking, and planning career changes. Discover ways to increase your earning power, from negotiating a raise to evaluating side hustle options that suit your goals.

Filled with illustrations, charts, and templates, *Own Your Money* is designed to be a hands-on companion as you build a strong and successful financial future.
